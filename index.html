Sensitivity controls how fast your screen moves when you swipe. Higher sensitivity means faster movement, while lower sensitivity gives better control.

Note: These settings may vary depending on your device and play style. Adjust them according to your comfort.

This is the most popular trick. While shooting, drag your fire button slightly upward to move the aim from body to head.

2. Use High Sensitivity

Higher sensitivity helps you move your aim quickly to the enemy’s head. Adjust it according to your comfort.

3. Crosshair Placement

Always keep your crosshair at head level while moving. This reduces the need for large adjustments during fights.

4. Choose the Right Guns

  • SMG (MP40, UMP) for close range
  • AR (AK, M4A1) for mid range
  • Sniper (AWM) for long range

5. Burst Fire Control

Don’t hold the fire button continuously. Use short bursts to control recoil and improve accuracy.

6. Practice in Training Ground

Spend time in training mode to improve your aim and reaction speed.

7. Use Aim Assist Properly

Keep aim assist ON. It helps your aim lock closer to the enemy, making headshots easier.

8. Stay Calm During Fights

Don’t panic. Calm aim increases your accuracy and chances of hitting headshots.
